# Privacy Policy for IndiFit

**Effective Date:** July 20, 2026  
**App Version:** 1.0.0

IndiFit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal health and fitness privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how IndiFit handles your data.

---

## 1. 100% On-Device Data Storage (Offline-First)

- **Local Storage:** All food search history, logged meals, custom recipes, active workouts, routine templates, and body weight/measurement logs are stored **strictly on your physical device** in a local SQLite database using encrypted storage capabilities.
- **No Remote Database:** IndiFit v1 does not upload, sync, or transmit your health, workout, or nutritional logs to any remote IndiFit servers or third-party cloud databases.

---

## 2. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Processing

- **AI Features:** Optional AI-powered features (such as AI Meal Estimation, AI Meal Planner, and AI Routine Generator) send text descriptions or user-uploaded food images to Google Gemini API endpoints solely to calculate nutrition or generate workout routines.
- **Zero Storage of Photos:** Images and meal text sent for AI estimation are processed statelessly and are **never stored or retained** after the estimation response is returned.

---

## 3. Health Connect & HealthKit Integration

- **On-Device Health Sync:** If you opt into integrating Health Connect (Android) or HealthKit (iOS), step counts, burned calories, and completed workouts are read from and written to your device's native health app **on-device only**.
- **No Transit:** Health platform data never transits IndiFit servers.

---

## 4. Diagnostics & Crash Telemetry

- **Optional Telemetry:** Anonymous diagnostic crash logs (if enabled by you in Settings) are processed with strict privacy controls to help us fix stability issues. Diagnostic reports contain zero personal identifiers, names, or food logs.

---

## 5. User Control & Data Export

- **Full Export:** You can export all your data as an encrypted JSON backup file or CSV spreadsheet at any time via Settings.
- **Complete Deletion:** Deleting the app or clearing local app storage permanently erases all IndiFit database records from your device.
